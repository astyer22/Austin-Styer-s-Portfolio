Web Development Projects

Why Web Development

I've always had a passion for art, but translating my creative visions from mind to paper has never been my strong suit. Drawing and painting have always eluded me in terms of skill. However, web development has become my creative outlet. It allows me to bring my ideas to life using technology.

Below are three websites I created using VSCode as my programming editor. Enjoy!

Sasquatch Mayhem

Sasquatch Mayhem is crafted for believers, skeptics, and the curious alike, inviting all to explore the mystery behind one of the world's most enduring legends – Bigfoot. The platform dives deep into this elusive phenomenon, blending folklore, scientific investigation, and eyewitness accounts to illuminate this captivating enigma. The site features an interactive homepage, a comprehensive "What is Bigfoot?" section, an image gallery, and a page dedicated to individual encounters, where users can also share their own experiences via email.

Scoots

The primary goal of this website project is to promote the rental services of Scoots, a fictional rental store. The website aims to provide comprehensive information about Scoots' services, requirements, and rental process. It features dedicated pages for available rentals, reservations, and an easy-to-use contact page to ensure a seamless experience for customers.

Visit Site

Chamber of Commerce Project

For this project, I was tasked with creating a website for a fictitious Chamber of Commerce. Inspired by the beloved Pokémon game, I designed the website to reflect a vibrant and interactive community. The site features a dynamic home page, a directory showcasing the esteemed members of the chamber, a dedicated section for community members to join the Chamber of Commerce, and a lively events page highlighting the latest happenings in the city.
